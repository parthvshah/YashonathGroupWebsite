Atoms and Computers by Prof. Michele Parrinello

February 17, 2020 - 4PM, MRC Auditorium

Prof. Michele Parrinello is an Italian physicist famously known for his contribution in molecular dynamics. He's won several awards including the Dirac Medal, the Sidney Fernbach Award in 2009, the Dreyfus Prize in the Chemical Sciences in 2017 and the Benjamin Franklin Medal (Franklin Institute) in 2020. He is going to give a lecture on the 17th of February, 2020 in the MRC auditorium, Indian Institute of Science, titled 'Atoms and Computers'.
